Idaho BLM Twin Falls District

The Twin Falls District is located in South-Central Idaho and manages approximately 3.9 million acres of public land. Home to 16 Type 4 Heavy Wildland Engines, three Osh Kosh Super Heavy Wildland Fire Engines, one Tatra Super Heavy Engine/Tactical Water Tender, three Type 6 Wildland Fire Engines, three Dozers, three Water Tenders, the Twin Falls Helitack crew and contracted Type 2 Helicopter, as well as the South Idaho Interagency Type 2 Initial Attack Crew. Each fire season BLM firefighters respond to on average 150 initial attack fires burning over 200,000 acres of rangeland and forest. This webpage is intended to serve as a resource for the public to use in gaining information about location and size of past and ongoing wildland fire incidents.

This webpage and map is to serve as proof of concept using the following five fires:

  • Murphy Complex Fire (2007)
  • Laidlaw Fire (2016)
  • Centennial Fire (2017)
  • Antelope Fire (2017)
  • Mammoth Fire (2017)
